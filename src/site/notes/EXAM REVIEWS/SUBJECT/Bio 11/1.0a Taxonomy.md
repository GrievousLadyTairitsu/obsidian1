- When naming species, make sure to write Genus with a capital and *species* with italicization or underlining
#### Taxonomical Relationships
##### Anatomical Relationships
- Relation of organisms regarding bone structure and arrangements
##### Physiological Relationships
- Relation of organism regarding their functions with similar molecules
##### Genetic Relationships
- Relation of organism regarding their Genus
	- DNA sequence determines everything
#### Taxonomical Graphs
#### Dichotomous Key
- dichotomous (dividing in two)
- A yes or no tree of characteristics of organisms
#### Phylogenetic Trees
- Hypothesizing the evolutionary relationship of similar organisms, based off sharing a common ancestor
	- Shows differences in time periods
#### Cladogram
- Does not imply time
- Compares organisms based on similar characteristics
- Distance between organisms shows how much difference they have between each other
