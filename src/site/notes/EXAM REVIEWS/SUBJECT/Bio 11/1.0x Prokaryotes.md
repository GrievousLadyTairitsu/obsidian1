# Prokaryotes

[[EXAM REVIEWS/SUBJECT/Bio 11/1.3b Bacteria\|1.3b Bacteria]]
[[EXAM REVIEWS/SUBJECT/Bio 11/1.4a Archaea\|1.4a Archaea]]
#### Properties of Prokaryotes
- Mainly unicellular
- Size: 1-10 $\mu \text{m}$
- Does not have a membrane-bound nucleus
	- Genetic material found in nucleoid
- 3 Shapes:
	- Coccus (Spherical)
	- Bacillus (Rod)
	- Spirillium (spirals)
- Chromosomes
	- Large circular DNA, defines the organism
- Plasmids
	- Small circular DNA that provides extra information for an organism
- Reproduction:
	- Binary Fission (Asexual)
	- May sexually reproduce through conjugation
		- Exchange of DNA (Induce by plasmid)*
- Organelle Behaviour
	- Not membrane bound organelles
