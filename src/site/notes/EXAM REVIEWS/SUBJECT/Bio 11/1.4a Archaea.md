# 1.4a Archaea
#### Properties
- Archaea live in extreme conditions
- Genetically closer to eukaryotes over bacteria
##### Methanogens
- Archaea organisms that produce methane and exist in oxygen-free environment
##### Halophiles 
- Archaea organisms living in salt water environments (~15% salt water)
##### Thermo-acidophiles
- Archaea organisms living in high heat acidic environments
##### Psychrophiles/Cryophile 
- Archaea organisms that live in very cold environments
