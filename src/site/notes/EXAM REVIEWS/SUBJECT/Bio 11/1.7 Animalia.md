# Animalia
#### Animal Details
- Eukaryotic and multicellular
- Do not contain cell walls
- Heterotrophs that ingest their food
- Are motile - require
- energy to move
- Reproduce sexually
#### Classifications
- 1. **Levels of organization
	- Animals can be differentiated based on tissues and organs
